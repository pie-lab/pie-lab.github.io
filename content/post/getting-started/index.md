## Welcome!

Thank you for visiting my website. I'm a PhD student in Psychology at University Name, where I work with Prof. Advisor Name studying social and health psychology.

## My Research Interests

My research examines how psychological and social factors influence health and well-being. I'm particularly interested in:

- **Personality and health**: How individual differences shape health behaviors and outcomes
- **Social relationships**: The role of social support in buffering stress and promoting resilience
- **Health behavior change**: Developing effective interventions tailored to individual characteristics
- **Longitudinal methods**: Understanding how these processes unfold over time

## Why I Do This Work

I'm passionate about understanding the psychological mechanisms that promote health and well-being because this knowledge can inform interventions that actually help people. My goal is to conduct rigorous, open science that makes a real difference in people's lives.
